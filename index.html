About the Workshop

Not a workshop.
A talent discovery system.

Most students never get the chance to discover what they are truly capable of in Mathematics and Science. These workshops exist to change that.

Designed as an introduction to Olympiad-level thinking, our program goes far beyond the classroom syllabus. We don't teach students to memorise formulas — we teach them to understand where those formulas come from, so deeply that they can derive or recreate them from first principles. A student who truly understands an idea never forgets it.

Through carefully designed puzzles, group challenges, and discovery-based sessions, students learn to approach unfamiliar problems with curiosity rather than fear. When a child explains their reasoning to another child, something powerful happens — their understanding deepens and their confidence grows permanently.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

The philosophy that drives everything we build.

Program Structure

3 Days. 9 Hours.
A Lifetime of Thinking Differently.

Each day builds progressively — from discovering patterns, to strategic logic, to genuine Olympiad-level reasoning.

1
Day OneThe Mystery of Patterns
Students discover that mathematics is the science of patterns hiding everywhere around us. The goal is to replace math fear with curiosity — showing that thinking is more powerful than memorising.
  • The 9-Dots Challenge — thinking outside limits
  • Visual pattern discovery & sequences
  • Fibonacci in nature — the wow moment
  • Puzzle Sprint — 10 problems in 10 minutes
  • Secret of Odd Numbers — math as beauty
  • Team Chocolate Game — logic over guessing
2
Day TwoStrategy & Logical Thinking
Students learn to think strategically using parity, invariants, and combinatorics. Every activity reveals how systematic thinking beats brute force every single time.
  • Parity logic — odd, even, winning positions
  • Invariant thinking — what never changes
  • Combinatorics puzzles — counting cleverly
  • Team strategy competitions
  • Number theory introduction
  • Brain escape room challenge
3
Day ThreeOlympiad Thinking Exposure
Students experience genuine Olympiad-style problems — simplified but intellectually real. The grand finale competition surfaces top talent, and every student receives their personalised Talent Report.
  • Simplified IOQM-style problems
  • Modular arithmetic — remainder magic
  • Proof thinking — why, not just how
  • Grand puzzle championship
  • Leaderboard finals & recognition
  • Talent scorecard distribution to parents

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents need to know before registering.

Who can attend this workshop?▲
The workshop is designed for students in Class 5 to Class 7 (ages 10–13). These are the ideal years — curiosity is high, fear of mathematics hasn't yet formed, and cognitive habits are most malleable. Students with no prior Olympiad exposure are very welcome.
Is prior Olympiad experience required?▼
Not at all. This workshop is specifically designed as a first exposure to Olympiad thinking. Students with no prior experience often show the most dramatic breakthroughs because the approach is completely different from what they've seen in school.
What is the Olympiad Talent Index (OTI)?▼
The OTI is a scientifically designed scoring system that evaluates 5 cognitive skills across all 3 days: logical reasoning, pattern recognition, creative thinking, speed solving, and persistence. Each student is scored, normalised against all students assessed nationally, and parents receive a detailed report with skill scores, strengths, and a personalised recommendation.
What happens after the 3-day camp?▼
Every student receives a Talent Report Card. The top 15% of students by OTI score receive a personal invitation to join the 12-week Olympiad Discovery Program — a structured foundation course covering number theory, combinatorics, and mathematical reasoning at a deeper level.
What is included in the ₹499 fee?▼
The ₹499 fee covers 9 hours of facilitated learning across 3 days, a printed student workbook, puzzle kit and activity materials, a personalised Olympiad Talent Index report, a completion certificate, and your child's place in the national talent database.
Which cities is the workshop available in?▼
The pilot launch in June–July 2026 will be available in Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kochi, and Delhi. We will expand to 50+ cities in Phase 2 based on pilot outcomes.
How is this different from other coaching workshops?▼
Most workshops focus on tricks and activities to create excitement. We focus on thinking. Every session is built around discovery, not delivery. Every activity measures a cognitive skill. Every student walks away knowing something about how their own mind works — and every parent receives a report to prove it. We also offer a clear multi-level pathway from Class 5 through Olympiad training and JEE Advanced.
